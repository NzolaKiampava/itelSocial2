Thank you for trying Wondertag theme. The theme is elegant, clean and material design
and so much easy to setup.


Installing Theme:

----> Open the Skin folder.
----> There you will see "Themes" folder.
----> Just open "Themes" folder.
----> Copy "wondertag" folder.
----> Paste it in the theme directory of your website.
----> Then, activate the theme through the Admin Panel.
